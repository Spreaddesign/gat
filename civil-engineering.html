Driving Innovation in Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering at Global Academy of Technology offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to equip students with a strong foundation in various core and specialised areas of civil engineering. The program includes subjects and its applications in engineering from fundamental and major domains like Structural Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering, Transportation Engineering, Fluid Mechanics.
