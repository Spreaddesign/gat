Programs Details

Upon completing the B.Tech in Cyber-Physical Systems students will

Demonstrate technical proficiency in sensing, communication, computation, and controls for designing intelligent cyber-physical solutions.

Develop scalable and secure integrated systems combining hardware, software, and real-time data processing.

Excel in interdisciplinary research in areas like cybersecurity, sustainability, automation, and safety.

Apply CPS engineering principles to address challenges in industry, healthcare, gaming, and smart infrastructure.

Pursue industry roles or postgraduate studies in emerging technologies such as IoT, AI, and robotics.
